No Objection Certificate (NOC)

Application Assistance

Service:

We assist students in applying for a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the Tanzania Commission for Universities (TCU) before traveling abroad for studies.

Benefits:

  • Confirms the selected university and program are recognized.
  • Prevents future qualification recognition problems.
  • Facilitates visa processing and scholarship requirements.
Get Assistance

Recognition Certificate

Application Assistance

Service:

We assist graduates who completed their studies abroad in applying for a Recognition Certificate from the Tanzania Commission for Universities (TCU).

Benefits:

  • Ensures the degree is officially recognized in Tanzania.
  • Allows employment in both public and private sectors.
  • Enables eligibility for further studies in Tanzania.
